Transcript  ·  Confidential
Wine has been poured. Christina stands up.

"Okay. I've been working on something. I want to run it by you three." She puts her phone face-down.

"It's a product company. Premium positioning. Norwegian brand. A personal care device with interchangeable heads."

She looks at Unn Mari. "You know how much you spend on your skincare ritual. This is that level of commitment, but for... a different part of the ritual." Unn Mari nods. She doesn't know yet.

"Gunn — you need more quality. Less compromise. And your hip will genuinely thank you." Gunn tilts her head. Getting warmer.

"Titti — you use your mouth professionally. This is just expanding the scope."

"The morning head is for oral care. Very good. Very precise."

Beat.

"The evening head is..." She picks up the device from the table. "...for relaxation."

She holds it out. She is not smiling.

Titti gets it first. Then Gunn. Then Unn Mari.

The sounds they make: a sharp inhale, a wheeze, and the specific Norwegian noise that means "absolutely not" and "give me that" at the same time.

"I've already ordered four." She sits down, pours wine.
"I'll expense them as team wellness."

They will talk about this for years. Christina knows this. That's why she did it.
